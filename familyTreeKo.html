Tae-woo Ko

Born June 18, 1935 on Jeju Island, Korea (now South Korea).

Japanese name is Kenjirou Takamura.

Married Kyong-Ja Kim on November 4, 1959 in Osaka, Japan.

Occupation was restaurant manager.

Hakusu Ko

Born January 8, 1961 in Osaka, Japan.

Japanese name is Yoshio Takamura.

Graduated from Keio University in Tokyo.

Occupation is manager at Tokyo Electronic Ltd.; soon to retire.

Sunghee Ko

Born September 9. 1962 in Osaka, Japan.

Japanese name is Chieko Takamura.

Graduated from Nihon University in Tokyo and Parsons School of Design (where she was valedictorian) in New York, New York in 1992.

Married George Alfred Greenslade III on December 17, 1999 in New York, New York.

Naturalized as a US citizen on February 27, 2015 in New York, New York.

Occupation was in interior design and retail sales.

Mother of the best son ever.

Poesy

When dogged stress within me coils itself
And fearsome matters weigh upon my mind;
When without all is dreary, bleak and dark
And one and all seek respite from the cold
I hie myself to her place succor for.
There in a cozy room abides my love
Whose arms me welcome and whose lips me greet
With passionate ardor that I find so sweet.

Some solace seek in drink or church or drug;
Still others yearn for family's warm embrace.
In discontented times I may be found
In one spot only - that is Sunghee's place.
1996

Has anyone ever been kissed
As much as Sunghee's been kissed by me?
Has ever there been skin so soft,
As smoothly soft as skin can be?
Has ever the ocean run dry
Or the dry desert lacked for sand?

How many meters separate
This earth from heaven's yellow ball?
How many snowy flakes
On a winter's solstice fall?
No more than the times our lips
In yearning have sweetly met
And no more than the times my lips
Kisses on her cheeks I hope to set.
1997
