Poesy
\n \n
\n
\n \t\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\n \t
\n \t

\n \t

When dogged stress within me coils itself
\n \t And fearsome matters weigh upon my mind;
\n \t When without all is dreary, bleak and dark
\n \t And one and all seek respite from the cold
\n \t I hie myself to her place succor for.
\n \t There in a cozy room abides my love
\n \t Whose arms me welcome and whose lips me greet
\n \t With passionate ardor that I find so sweet.
\n \t
\n \t Some solace seek in drink or church or drug;
\n \t Still others yearn for family's warm embrace.
\n \t In discontented times I may be found
\n \t In one spot only - that is Sunghee's place.
\n \t 1996

\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t

\n\t\t\t\t\t\t

Has anyone ever been kissed
\n\t\t\t\t\t\tAs much as Sunghee's been kissed by me?
\n\t\t\t\t\t\tHas ever there been skin so soft,
\n\t\t\t\t\t\tAs smoothly soft as skin can be?
\n\t\t\t\t\t\tHas ever the ocean run dry
\n\t\t\t\t\t\tOr the dry desert lacked for sand?
\n\t\t\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\tHow many meters separate
\n\t\t\t\t\t\tThis earth from heaven's yellow ball?
\n\t\t\t\t\t\tHow many snowy flakes
\n\t\t\t\t\t\tOn a winter's solstice fall?
\n\t\t\t\t\t\tNo more than the times our lips
\n\t\t\t\t\t\tIn yearning have sweetly met
\n\t\t\t\t\t\tAnd no more than the times my lips
\n\t\t\t\t\t\tKisses on her cheeks I hope to set.
1997
