George Greenslade

","\t\t\t\t\t

Born about 1832 in Rosh Ash, county of Devon, England. Various sources have the birth date from 1831 to 1837.

","\t\t\t\t\t

Married Jane Baker on May 4, 1861 in Independent Chapel in South Molton, Devon.

","\t\t\t\t\t

","\t\t\t\t\t

Occupation was given at various times as butcher and as cattle dealer.

Died October 7, 1886 at 69 East Street in South Molton, Devon of phthisis.

","\t\t\t\t\t

Buried October 13, 1886 at St. Mary's Church in Bishop's Nympton, Devon.
