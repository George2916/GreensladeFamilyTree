\"Ruth Elizabeth Longinetti, 70, of Jay, N.Y., passed away Friday, May 13, 2011 at CVPH Medical Center. Ruth was born Feb. 10, 1941, in Bronx, New York, to Willard and Florence Guinn. There she met and married her loving husband Ray for the past 48 years. She was the best mother that anyone could ask for to her children and to all others she took under her wing. Ruthie loved her gardening and was avid motorcycle rider with Ray and was a member of the HOGS groups and the America Legion Riders.

Ruth is survived by her husband Ray Longinetti of Jay, her sisters Dolores Forkin and Mary Stone, both of Pa. Ruthie has five surviving children, Elizabeth Theresa Bishop of Juneau, Alaska, Donna Longinetti, Michael Longinetti, both of New Jersey, and Anthony Longinetti of North Carolina. Ruth has 11 grandchildren Daniel, Christopher, Robert and Stephen Bishop, Heather, Samantha, Michael Jr. Longinetti and Alyson, Maxwell Huelster, Alexis and Nicholas Longinetti. She is also survived by four great-grandsonsons, Caden, Justin, Cameron and Aiden Bishop and Baby Bishop to be born June 21.

She will always be remembered for her loving and caring ways along with her outgoing personality to all. Ruthie touched the lives of so many people within the community she lived and adored everyone around her. Her zeal and compassion for life and caring, loving compassion to all will be greatly missed but remembered. A Mass of Christian burial will be held at 11 a.m. Friday May 20, 2011, at Holy Name Church in AuSable Forks. Burial will follow in Holy Name Cemetery. Arrangements are in the care of Zaumetzer-Sprague Funeral Home. In lieu of flowers and gifts, we ask that you make a donation to the Hospice of North Country. Online condolences may be offered at www.pressrepublican.com.\"
