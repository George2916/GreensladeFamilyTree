Mary \"Mae\" Ellen Torpey
\n \n
\n
\n \t\n\t\t\t\t\t\n \t
\n \t

\n \t

Born July 11, 1908 in Sale, county of Cheshire, England.

\n \t

Her father died when she was very young and her mother a few years later. After her mother's death, she lived with relatives in County Roscommon, Ireland before emigrating to the United States, where she lived with one of her mother's sisters, all three of whom had previously moved to the United States.\n \t

\n \t

Died May 13, 2004 in New York, New York.

\n \t

Buried in St. Raymond's Cemetery in the Bronx, New York in same plot as Edward Joseph Duffy (husband) and Patrick O. Torpey (brother).
