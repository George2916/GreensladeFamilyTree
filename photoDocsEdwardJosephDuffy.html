Edward Joseph Duffy

Birth Certificate

Extract from the 1930 census of Manhattan, New York. The pertinent information is on line 45.
The census indicates that Edward Josepeh Duffy, age 21, was a lodger at the residence of Mary Keny (née McDonagh), as was his sister, Cathleen Duffy (see line 46). Later on, in 1934, Edward was to marry Mary "Mae" Ellen Torpey, the neice of Mary Keny. Mae's brother, Patrick "Packy" O. Torpey, was also lodger with Mary Keny (see line 40).
