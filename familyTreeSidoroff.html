Theodore Sidoroff

Born in Russia in approximately 1898.

Extract from 1930 census of Fell Township, Pennsylvania. ↗
The census indicates that in 1930 the Sidoroff houseold consisted of Theodore (head, age 32), Elinor (wife, age 33), John (son, age 6 months), Anthony Putnick (step son, age 4), Mary Malounas (step daughter, age 9) and Jennie Malounas (step daughter, age 6).

John Thomas Sidoroff

Graduated from New York University.

Served as private first class in the U.S. army.

Occupation was banker.

Buried in Arizona Veterans Memorial Cemetery in Marana, Arizona.
