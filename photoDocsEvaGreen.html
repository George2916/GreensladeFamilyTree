Eva Josephine Green

"Mrs. Eva Greenslade Dies At Home Here", The Salisbury Times (Maryland), December 21,1946
The article reads as follows:
     A Low Mass of Requiem will be said by Rev. Eugene T. Stout in St. Francis de Sales Catholic Church here Tuesday at 9:30 a.m. for Mrs. Eva J. Greenslade, who died last night at her home on Hammond St. Burial will be in Wicomico Memorial Park.
     Mrs. Greenslade is survived by her husband, George A. Greenslade; four sons, Robert, of Salisbury; John, of Atlanta, Ga; and James and Richard of New York City; four daughters, Mrs. E.S. Towery, Concord, N.C.; Mrs. O.C. Burnett, Atlanta, Ga; and Misses Carolina and Georgiana Greenslade, of Salisbury. Several grandchildren also survive.
     Friends may call at the Holloway Funeral Home Monday after 7:30 p.m.
