"Death of Infant", Augusta Herald (Georgia), August 3, 1921
The article reads as follows:
     Announcement is made of the death of the infant son of Mr. and Mrs. William Greenslade, which occurred Wednesday at 5:30 A.M. at the residence, 1941 Walton Way. Deceased was 24 days old.
     Funeral services will be conducted at the residence Thursday at 10:00 A.M., Rev. S.L. McCarty officiating, and interment will follow at the City Cemetery.
