Eugene Sanford Towery

Oakwood Cemetery, Concord, North Carolina
This plot contains the graves of three generations of Towerys: Eugene Sanford Towery (b. 1884), Bessie Greenslade, Genevieve Towery, Eugene Sanford Towery (b. 1919), Mary Dell Long, Caroline Elizabeth Towery, John William Towery and Ann Louise Towery.
