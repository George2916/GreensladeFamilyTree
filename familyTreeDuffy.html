Kathleen Duffy

Her birth record gives her name as Catherine but the 1950 census of Queens, New York gives her name as Kathleen.

Catherine Duffy was born on September 29, 1909 in Ballaghaderreen, County Roscommon, Ireland, the daughter of Michael Duffy and Ann Duffy (née Hennigan).

She died on May 25, 1978 in Rockaway Beach, New York.

She married James Joseph Coburn. They had two children, Margaret ("Peggy") and James.

James Joseph Coburn was born on August 16, 1902 in County Louth, Ireland.

James Joseph Coburn died on May 29, 1978 in Rockaway Beach, New York.

Mary "Mae" Ellen Torpey

Born July 11, 1908 in Sale, county of Cheshire, England.

Her father died when she was very young and her mother a few years later. After her mother's death, she lived with relatives in County Roscommon, Ireland before emigrating to the United States, where she lived with one of her mother's sisters, all three of whom had previously moved to the United States.

Died May 13, 2004 in New York, New York.

Buried in St. Raymond's Cemetery in Bronx, New York in same plot as Edward Joseph Duffy (husband) and Patrick "Packy" O. Torpey (brother).
