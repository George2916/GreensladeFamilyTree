Albert Morrison

Notice of death, newspaper unknown

"ALBERT MORRISON, lawyer, died at his residence, 823 Buena avenue, yesterday of pneumonia at the age of 46. He was a member of the Union League club, the Elks, and the Chicago Bar association. He leaves a widow and two children."

Notice of death, newspaper unknown

"MORRISON—Albert Morrison, at his home, 813 Buena av., beloved husband of Jessie Kinnian Morrison, fond father of Albert and Eleanor. Member of Chicago lodge No. 4, B.P.O.E. Funeral arrangements later."
